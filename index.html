Game Overview

Wise Hero is an engaging puzzle game that tests your strategic thinking and planning abilities. The game challenges you to combine identical tiles to create higher-valued ones and aim for the highest score possible. With simple controls but deep strategy, it's perfect for quick gaming sessions or extended play.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or swipe gestures to move all tiles in one direction
  • When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one tile with double the value
  • Plan your moves carefully to avoid getting stuck with no valid moves
  • Reach the legendary 2048 tile to win, or challenge yourself to go even further!
